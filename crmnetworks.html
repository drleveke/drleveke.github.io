The roots of this software go back to ACE Air in Phoenix, where David Meyers (Cody‚Äôs father) and programmer Mike Scouten built the first version of what would become Ticket Writer. SMW Refrigeration ‚Äì Ticket Writer 2.0 (2013)

In 2013, Cody Leveke built Ticket Writer 2.0 at SMW Refrigeration & Heating. It combined VICIdial with vTiger CRM, using custom PHP webforms that prefilled lead data and automated appointment scheduling. Agents could book jobs without overfilling slots, and cancelled calls automatically reopened the schedule.

Cody also developed Bidder, a mobile-friendly tool that let techs generate real-time replacement quotes, factoring in payment methods (cash, card, financing fees) and target margins. This eliminated endless phone calls from the field and gave techs pricing power in their hands.

At SMW, Ticket Writer was also integrated with QuickBooks Enterprise. A MySQL mirror of QB allowed automated lead reactivation (customers overdue for maintenance were auto-added into VICIdial call lists) and commission automation (20% service, 8% installs, bonuses after thresholds, only paid when QB marked invoice as paid). What once took an accountant a week became a one-button process.
