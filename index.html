Wrongfully Convicted as a Juvenile

I was convicted in adult court for something I did as a juvenile. The law is clear — what happened was legally a delinquent act, not a felony. I should have never been charged in adult court. My attorneys at the time failed to raise the issue, and the court got it wrong.

This conviction is not legally valid, and the State of Iowa is now being forced to revisit it. A post-conviction trial is scheduled where I will finally have the opportunity to clear my name.

  • Date: September 9, 2025
  • Judge: McLeland
  • Court: Iowa District Court for Polk County
  • Case No.: PCCE09046
